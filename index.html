Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how AdStock (the “App”), operated by AdStock.ai ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our services, including integrations with Google user data via OAuth and related APIs. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Data: Name, email address, profile picture, and unique Google user ID obtained via OAuth scopes.
  • Ad Performance Data: Campaign metrics from Google Ads and Meta Ads (e.g. impressions, clicks, spend, conversions, CPC, CTR).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, pages visited, feature usage, timestamps, and error logs.
  • Support & Communication Data: Emails, feedback, and form submissions you provide when contacting our support team.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect data directly from you when you:

  • Grant access via Google OAuth during login or consent screen.
  • Use our dashboard to connect advertising accounts.
  • Submit forms or communicate with us.

3. Use of Google User Data

We use Google user data only to:

  • Provision and manage your AdStock account and connected ad campaigns.
  • Fetch and display ad performance metrics in our dashboard.
  • Automate budget adjustments and optimizations using AI-driven analytics.
  • Send activation, notification, and marketing emails with your consent.

We do not sell or share your Google user data with data brokers or for targeted advertising outside the scope of our service.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who support hosting, email delivery (e.g. EmailJS), analytics (e.g. Google Analytics), and infrastructure (e.g. Google Cloud Platform), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or to respond to valid legal processes.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice and protections for your data rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal obligations. Unless required otherwise, we delete or anonymize data older than 24 months from last activity.

6. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication for internal systems.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Incident response procedures to address any suspected data breach.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing).
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

8. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top and notify you via email or in-app notification.
